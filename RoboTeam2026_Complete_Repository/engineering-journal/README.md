# Engineering Journal

This journal documents the complete engineering process of the RoboTeamBZU WRO Future Engineers 2026 vehicle.

The purpose is not only to show the final robot, but also to explain:

- what was built,
- why each decision was made,
- what was tested,
- what failed,
- what changed,
- and how the final system can be reproduced.

## Documentation Rules Used by the Team

For every major change, record:

1. **Previous configuration**
2. **Problem or limitation**
3. **Proposed change**
4. **Reason for the change**
5. **Test method**
6. **Measured or observed result**
7. **Decision: keep, revise, or reject**

## Evidence to Include

- CAD screenshots and dimensioned drawings
- Robot photos before and after changes
- Wiring diagrams
- Power measurements
- Sensor placement diagrams
- Software flowcharts and state machines
- Test tables and videos
- Failure analysis and corrective actions
